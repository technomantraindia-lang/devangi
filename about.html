Our Journey

From Ground-Level Execution
to Integrated Power Solutions

Devangi Power Pvt. Ltd. began its journey in Surat with hands-on expertise in underground cabling and transmission line works. Built on technical understanding, disciplined field execution and a strong commitment to reliability, DPPL has grown into an EPC company delivering solutions across transmission infrastructure, substations, underground cable projects and industrial power distribution.

Today, DPPL continues to strengthen its capabilities with a safety-first approach, skilled workforce and an end-to-end execution model for evolving power infrastructure requirements.
